CHUO CHA TAKWIMU MASHARIKI MWA AFRIKA

The community of The Eastern Africa Statistical Training Center is delighted to welcome each one of you to the 7th convocation of the year 2024.
This convocation is held as a continuous tradition within the academic institutions of gathering together academicians, students, alumni, benefactors and industry professionals as stakeholders within the university community.Which will be held at The Eastern Africa Statistical Training Center in Changanyikeni.

THEME:Modernizing data ecosystems to accelerate the implementation of African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCTA): The role of official statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable developmet of Africa.
The event wil start from 3:00am to 15:30. And will take place within the EASCT grounds at Changanyikeni.
